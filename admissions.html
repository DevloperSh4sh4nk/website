Frequently Asked Questions

What is the student-teacher ratio at Wisdom School?

We maintain an excellent student-teacher ratio of 10:1, with a maximum of 25 students per class. This ensures personalized attention and a better learning experience for each student.

What are the school timings?

Our regular school timings are Monday to Friday from 7:50 AM to 3:50 PM. On Saturdays, we have Innovation Day from 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM, which includes hackathons, startup mentoring, and special workshops.

Do you provide transportation?

Yes, we provide safe and reliable transportation services with GPS-enabled buses and trained female attendants. Routes cover all major areas of Roorkee and nearby localities. Transportation fees vary based on distance.

What is the admission process?

Our admission process includes:

  1. Submission of the online application form
  2. Entrance assessment (age-appropriate)
  3. Interaction with the student and parents
  4. Review of previous academic records
  5. Admission decision within 48 hours of the interaction
Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships for exceptional students. Scholarships can cover up to 100% of tuition fees based on the entrance test performance and interview. We also have need-based financial aid for deserving students from underprivileged backgrounds.

What foreign languages are offered?

We offer three foreign language options: French, German, and Arabic. Students can choose one language to specialize in, with daily classes and opportunities to earn international certifications like DELF (French), Goethe-Zertifikat (German), or equivalent for Arabic.

How is the school handling COVID-19 safety?

We follow all government health guidelines, including regular sanitization, mandatory temperature checks, and social distancing measures in classrooms. Our hybrid learning model ensures continuity of education during any disruptions. All staff are fully vaccinated, and we strongly encourage vaccination for all eligible students.
