About Wisdom School

India's First AI & English Grammar Girls School

Our Story

Located in the peaceful and education-rich city of Roorkee, Uttarakhand, Wisdom School is India's first and only complete Artificial Intelligence + World-Class English school exclusively for girls. We are ICSE-affiliated and go far beyond the regular ICSE syllabus. While other schools teach Computer as one period a week, we make Artificial Intelligence the heartbeat of the entire curriculum.

From age 3, every girl learns to code, build AI tools, speak flawless English with a neutral accent, and earn real money online – all before she reaches Class 9.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To create India's most powerful generation of female AI entrepreneurs, scientists, and global leaders who will build billion-dollar companies and speak on the world's biggest stages – starting from Roorkee.

Our Mission

  • • Every girl financially independent through AI before Class 9
  • • Perfect London-accent English + IELTS 8+ band level by Class 8
  • • Professional working knowledge of one foreign language
  • • 100% girls ready for top IITs, Stanford, Oxford, and top ICSE Class 11 streams

Our Core Values

AI-First Education

Integrating AI across all subjects to prepare students for the future.

Girls' Empowerment

Creating confident, independent young women ready to lead in tech.

Global Perspective

Preparing students to compete and collaborate on a global scale.

Holistic Development

Nurturing academic excellence along with emotional and social growth.
